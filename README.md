I'm an AI and software engineer with a First-Class BSc in **Artificial Intelligence with Computer Science** from the University of Birmingham.

I'm currently the **Co-founder, CTO & Lead Software Engineer at Matched Stay**, where I build web and mobile products for corporate travel and long-term worker accommodation.

I like building useful systems from scratch: full-stack platforms, applied AI tools, automation workflows, research prototypes and products that move from vague idea to real users.

## Selected work

### Matched Stay

Co-founded and built a live travel-booking platform for managing long-term worker accommodation.

- Built web and mobile products with **Next.js**, **React Native**, **Node.js** and **Firebase**
- Owned technical delivery across product design, database design, APIs, deployment and release testing
- Worked directly with users, investors and business stakeholders to turn operational requirements into product decisions

### AI Drug Discovery Pipeline

Built an AI/ML research pipeline for molecule generation and property-aligned drug discovery.

- Used **ChemBERTa embeddings**, autoencoders, transformer models, reinforcement learning and supervised learning
- Used LLM-generated pharmacological labels to support downstream classifiers and reward functions
- Built a full-stack researcher-facing web application for experimenting with the pipeline

### Digital Chemistry Research

Worked on machine learning architectures for digital chemistry and computer vision.

- Experimented with autoencoders for molecular skeleton representations
- Reported findings to domain experts and produced technical research outputs for further experimentation
